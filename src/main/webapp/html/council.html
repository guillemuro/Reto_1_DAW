Responsibilities of the Committee


  • President of the Company Committee: In charge of representing the committee before the management of the cafeteria and leading the meetings of the Committee.

  • Secretary of the Company Committee: In charge of carrying the record of the minutes of the meetings and coordinating the communication between the committee and the employees of the coffee shop.

  • Responsible for Communication: Co-ordinate the external communication of the cafeteria, including social networks, advertising and relations with the media.

  • Human Resources Responsible: Manages the affairs related to the employees of the cafeteria, including hiring, training, evaluation and compensation.

  • Responsible for Finance: In charge of managing the financial affairs of the cafeteria, including accounting, budget and management of economic resources.

Shipping & Returns
Fast delivery and easy returns, because we value your time

Shipping

Welcome to our mailing page. In this section, we inform you about our shipping policies and the options available to receive your products from the coffee shop.

At our coffee shop, we take delivery of our products very seriously. Therefore, we work with the best shipping companies to ensure that your products arrive at their destination quickly and safely.

Here are the shipping options we offer:

  • Standard Shipping: This shipping method takes between 3 to 7 business days and is free for purchases exceeding a certain amount.

  • Express shipping: If you need to receive your products in a shorter period, we offer express shipping that takes between 1 to 2 business days. This service has an additional cost.

  • Collection in store: If you are in the area, you can also choose to collect your products directly in our cafeteria. This service is free of charge and will be available within 24 hours.
It is important to note that shipping times may vary depending on the location and weather conditions. In addition, we offer international shipments with additional costs.

We also care about the packaging of our products, ensuring that they are packaged safely and responsibly to protect the environment.

Thank you for choosing our coffee shop. Enjoy our coffee and sweets wherever you are!


Returns

In Ctrl Alt + Coffe, we strive to offer products of the best quality and taste, but we know that sometimes it may be necessary to make a return. Therefore, we ensure that we have a clear and easy to follow return policy.

If for any reason you are not satisfied with your order, please contact us as soon as possible. Here are the return options we offer:

  • Full refund of the amount of your order if the product does not meet your expectations or if you receive a damaged or in bad condition.
  • Change products if you prefer to choose another product from our coffee shop.

Here are the details of our return process:

  1. Contact us via our email or phone number, explaining the reason for your return.
  2. We will provide you with instructions on how to make the return and how to send us the product.
  3. Send the product in its original packaging and in perfect condition.
  4. Once you have received the product, we will refund you the full amount or send you the new product, as the case may be.
It is important to note that shipping and return costs are borne by the buyer, except in cases where the product has arrived damaged or in bad condition.

In case of any questions or questions about our return policies, please do not hesitate to contact us. Our team will be happy to help you with what you need.

Thank you for choosing our coffee shop. We hope you enjoy our products and we assure you that we will do everything possible to guarantee your satisfaction!

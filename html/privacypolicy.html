Privacy policy
Your privacy is as important as your morning coffee

March 2023

At Ctrl Alt + Coffee, we are committed to protecting your privacy when you use our online ordering system. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you make an order online from our coffee.


Information We Collect

When you place an order through our online system, we may collect the following personal information from you:

  • Your name.
  • Your contact information (phone number, email address, delivery address).
  • Your payment information (credit card details).
  • We may also collect information about your use of our website, such as your IP address, browser type, and device information.


    • How We Use Your Information

    We use your personal information to process your orders and deliver your food to you. We may also use your information to contact you about your orders or to send you marketing emails if you have opted in to receive them. We may use aggregated, non-personal information for statistical analysis and research purposes to improve our services.


    How We Share Your Information

    We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with third-party service providers who help us to process payments and deliver orders. We may also share your information with law enforcement agencies or other third parties if required by law or to protect our rights or the rights of our customers.


    Your Choices

    You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information that we hold. You can also opt-out of receiving marketing emails from us at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of our emails.


    Security

    We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. We use industry-standard encryption technology to protect your payment information.


    Changes to this Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.
